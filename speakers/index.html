  • Rorie Edmunds

    Rorie Edmunds is an independent scholar based in Tokyo with 15 years of experience leading and coordinating international initiatives in Research Data Management and infrastructure, scaling communities globally across scientific disciplines. He specializes in community engagement and communications; persistent identifiers (PIDs) and metadata; and promoting Open, FAIR, and trustworthy data practices. Rorie co-led the development of the CoreTrustSeal Certification Standard and is an author of the TRUST Principles for digital repositories. Rorie is currently contracted to the Australian Research Data Commons as the APAC Community and Partnership Specialist for RAiD (the Research Activity identifier). Prior to this, he was Samples Community Manager at DataCite, leading its partnership with the founding organization of the International Generic Sample Number and responsible for driving adoption of PIDs and the development of PID practice standards for material samples. Rorie was Executive Director of ISC’s World Data System from 2018 to 2021. Rorie’s research background is highly multidisciplinary. His first degree is Mathematics, and he holds a PhD in Structural Geology.

  • 呂佳陵

    國立東華大學自然資源學系 研究助理

    講者目前負責 LTSER 花蓮站之資料管理相關工作，協助整理與維護長期研究計畫中所產生的各類資料，包含自然生態監測資料與社會科學問卷調查資料。工作內容涵蓋資料結構整理、資料流程盤點、網站平台建置，以及資料備份與長期保存方式之規劃，並實際參與跨研究團隊之資料協調與溝通。在花蓮站的資料管理實務中，需同時面對不同資料型態、研究尺度與管理習慣所帶來的挑戰，例如高度結構化的生態監測資料，以及涉及隱私與使用限制的社會科學調查資料。目前正逐步盤點計畫內各子計畫的資料管理現況，嘗試在不影響研究進行的前提下，建立可長期維護的基本管理原則。本次分享將介紹花蓮站目前資料管理的實際作法與初步規劃方向，並透過工作坊交流，討論不同站點在多元資料管理上的經驗與可能做法。
